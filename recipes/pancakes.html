Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a perfect for when you crave something sweet.

They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cut flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour and eggs.
  2. Gradually add the milk and water.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
